ABOUT US

Biokarfred medical laboratory provides basic medical laboratory investigations for:
‣ Private and public hospitals.
‣ Private patients.
‣ We also participate in NGOs programmes such as Tuberculosis eradication, HIV and Malaria awareness campaign.
And we partake in MLSC quality control programme.
